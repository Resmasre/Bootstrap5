Women's Size Chart

Find your perfect fit with our detailed size guide

How to Measure

Use a flexible measuring tape to take your measurements. Measure around the fullest part of your bust, waist, and hips. Make sure the tape is level and not too tight.

Size Bust (inches) Waist (inches) Hips (inches)
XS 32-33 24-25 34-35
S 34-35 26-27 36-37
M 36-37 28-29 38-39
L 38-40 30-32 40-42
XL 41-43 33-35 43-45
Tip: If you are between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.
Note: Measurements are in inches. For the best fit, take your measurements while wearing undergarments similar to those you will wear with the final outfit.
